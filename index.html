NEUROTIC NEURONS:
AN INTERACTIVE EXPLANATION.

For a while now, I've been having the mind on my mind. I used to suffer from a lot of anxiety (well, I still do) so I made this to help peeps understand their brain. Via Learning-By-Doing™, hopefully it can get you to realize that while it's hard to rewire yourself, it is possible.

If you're a huge nerd, here's some Wikipedia articles to the psychology/neuroscience concepts covered in Neurons. I list them here also to assure you I wasn't making stuff up.
  • Conditioning
  • Hebbian Learning
  • Anti-Hebbian Learning
  • Exposure Therapy
